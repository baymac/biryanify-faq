What time can I place order?

We prefer to take orders 1 day in advance. Although you can order before 9 am for Lunch and before 4pm for dinner.

Why orders are taken in advance?

We want to provide fresh & hot food with no reused oil or preserved ingredients. So we prepare food specifically for your order.

Is there in extra delivery charges?

For Sambalpur town, delivery fee is Rs. 30  For Burla and Hirakud, delivery fee is Rs. 50

Where is your service available?

We are currently only operating within Sambalpur town, Hirakud and Burla.

How to place an order in website?

1. Click on any product (e.g. Mutton Biryani)
2. Click on add to cart
3. Click on edit, type the number of plates you want
4. Click on update
5. Select the date on which you want delivery
6. Click on check out and proceed with the instructions on your screen

How to create an account?

1. Click on account button in homepage.
2. Click on create an account 
3. Set your name, email and password.
4. Click on create

Why do I need an account?

You need an account for faster checkout, it saves your card and address information. Also you can also see your order history.

How to recover my forgotten password?

1. Click on account button in homepage. 
2. Click on forgot your password? 
3. Enter your email to get the reset link, click on submit
4. Click on reset link on your email
5. Set your new password

Is my data safe?

Yes, we respect our customers privacy and we do not share your information to any third party.
